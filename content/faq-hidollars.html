What are hi Dollars?

The hi Dollar is our membership token and a cryptocurrency used for accessing the services offered by hi. Members can earn or buy hi Dollars and then can access services and benefits based on their membership tier. Think of it as a membership token that grants you benefits but will be resellable should you decide to end your membership.

How do I get hi Dollars?

The first 100,000 members to sign up for hi will receive a 100 hi Dollars each day for engaging with us (e.g. saying “hi”). After 100,000 members, the rate will adjust to 10 hi Dollars per day and after 10 Million members, to 1 hi Dollar per day. Referring friends to hi will also earn you additional hi Dollars. You may purchase additional hi Dollars through hi by using the “Buy hi Dollars” command, by visiting hi.com/hi-dollars and ultimately, through a number of reputable digital currency exchange partners (details will follow in due course).

What are hi Dollars used for?

hi Dollars is our membership token. By earning and depositing hi Dollars long term, you are a member and thus, entitled to services that will be offered by hi. You may buy or earn hi Dollars to increase your membership tier with increased benefits.

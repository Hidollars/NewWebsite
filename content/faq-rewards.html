Flexible, Savings and Rewards - What are these?

Flexible: Your money is truly mobile. Conveniently send cryptocurrency to friends with our web application or one of the chat channels. All members who have undergone KYC are also free to deposit and withdraw their crypto. The roll out of fiat services in the future will allow you to move your money around just as easily. Cryptocurrencies in your Current Account are withdrawable anytime.

Savings: Earn interest for all deposits. The amount of hi Dollars locked in your Savings Account determines your membership tier. Both HI and other cryptocurrencies transferred into your Savings Account earn interest based on membership tier. Interest is paid daily in hi Dollars into the member’s Current Account. Cryptocurrencies transferred into Savings are locked for 12 months.

Rewards Account: Your bonus hi Dollars are here. Members are encouraged to engage daily with hi in order to earn their Daily Rewards (New Member and Referral Bonuses). Earned HI are deposited into your Rewards Account at day end. No interest is paid for these bonus hi Dollars and they do not count towards your membership tier. ‍hi Dollars in your Rewards Account will be released into Flexible after 12 months and KYC completion.
